Beef Tacos

Description

These crispy fried ground beef tacos are made by filling tortillas as you fry them, folding in cheese and taco meat, then carefully opening them to dress with lettuce, salsa, sour cream, and cilantro.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Corn Tortillas
  • Onion
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Tomato Paste
  • Cheese
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Cook Beef
  2. Season Meat
  3. Fry Tortillas
  4. Assemble Tacos
  5. Serve
